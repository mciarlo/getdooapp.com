  • How does Doo work?

    Doo is new way to get things done that isn’t boring or complicated. Instead Doo is simple by design. Each reminder you create becomes a card. Swipe a card up to mark it complete or down to snooze it for later. That’s it!

    The cards in Doo help you succeed. Showing only one task at a time lets you focus on a single goal without feeling overwhelmed. And each card is warm and inviting to give you that extra push you need to be successful.

  • How much does Doo cost?

    Doo is $4.99 on the App Store — less than the price of a beer.

    Pricing is often a tricky subject. Eyebrows go up when the cost of an app is higher than a dollar or two. That reaction has been conditioned into us, especially with in-app purchases hiding the true cost of many free apps — especially games.

    We believe the best apps are honest about their cost and put the user first. The best apps don't hide useful features behind paywalls. As a result Doo is a feature-rich tool that you can use for years to come. No user accounts, no sign-up forms, and no “pro” unlock required. Just a product you can be proud to have on your home screen.

  • What devices are supported?

    Doo is a universal app that supports iPhone, iPad, and iPod Touch. Actionable notifications allow you to complete or snooze reminders from your Apple Watch.

  • How can I see all my reminders or view reminders that aren't yet due?

    The schedule view shows current, upcoming, and completed reminders. Use this view to see upcoming tasks or manage to-dos. Open the schedule view by tapping the icon at the top right of the card screen.

  • How can I snooze a task until a specific day and time?

    Coming in version 1.0.1: Doo has a special feature you can use to bypass your default snooze duration. On iPhone 6s, force press the snooze button to select a day and time. On iPhone 6 and below, long press the snooze button to select a day and time.

  • Does Doo work with the iOS calendar or reminders app?

    Doo does not currently integrate with built-in iOS apps. We want Doo to be the best app for reminders and to-dos while preserving the simplicity that makes us special. This type of feature requires us to spend a bit more time to get it right. While there are no guarantees, this feature is under consideration.

  • Can I use my own images?

    Doo includes eighteen illustrations that bring warmth and personality to each card. These images are not currently customizable. If you think a subject or task isn’t represented, head over to our support page to make a request. We’re always looking for new illustrations!

  • Are there plans to build Doo for other platforms?

    We’d love to bring Doo to as many platforms as we can. While there are no official timelines, versions for Mac and Android are under consideration.
