  • How does Doo work?

    Doo is new way to get things done that isn’t boring or complicated. Instead Doo is simple by design. Each reminder you create becomes a card. Swipe a card up to mark it complete or down to snooze it for later. That’s it!

    The cards in Doo help you succeed. Showing only one task at a time lets you focus on a single goal without feeling overwhelmed. And each card is warm and inviting to give you that extra push you need to be successful.

  • How much does Doo cost?

    Doo is $4.99 for iOS and $9.99 for Mac. Together they offer world-class design and powerful functionality — all for less than the price of most productivity apps.

    We believe the best apps are honest about their cost and put the user first. The best apps don't hide useful features behind paywalls. Doo is a feature-rich tool that provides continuous updates for free. No user accounts, no sign-up forms, and no “pro” unlock required.

  • Why are iOS and Mac separate purchases?

    Doo for iOS and Mac unique codebases that require their own updates and maintenance. Each app also lives in a separate App Store. Your purchase goes towards supporting further development on each platform.

    Wherever possible, every effort is made to support as many devices as possible with a single purchase. That’s why Doo for iOS is universal and works on the iPad, iPhone, iPod Touch, and supports Apple Watch notifications. Doo for Mac supports all Mac devices runnning OS X Yosemite, OS X El Capitan, and macOS Sierra.

  • What devices are supported?

    Doo is available on iPhone, iPad, and iPod Touch as a universal app. You can also find Doo in the Mac App Store. Actionable notifications allow you to complete or snooze reminders from your Apple Watch.

  • Does Doo work with the iOS calendar or reminders app?

    Doo is meant to be a replacement for iOS reminders and does not currently integrate with built-in apps. We are actively investigating smart ways to integrate things like calendar events to make Doo even more helpful.

  • Can I use Doo with Siri?

    Doo and other third-party apps can’t integrate with Siri for things like reminder creation and dictation. We patiently wait for the day Apple announces improved Siri support.

  • Can I use my own images?

    Doo includes twenty-four illustrations that bring warmth and personality to each card. These images are not currently customizable. If you think a subject or task isn’t represented, head over to our support page to make a request. We’re always looking for new illustrations!

  • Are there plans to build Doo for other platforms?

    We’d love to bring Doo to as many platforms as we can. While there are no official timelines, versions for Web and Android are under consideration.
