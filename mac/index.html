How it works

Focus on a few cards each day. Build consistent progress over time.

Effortless task creation

Creating a task by typing in the title. Tap one of the icons above the keyboard to add dates, times, repeats, and more with a beautiful custom keyboard. Your reminder is written out like a sentence. Tap a word you’d like to change and the custom keyboard will re-appear. Simple.

Creating a task by typing in the title. Click one of the shortcuts to add dates, times, repeats, and more with beautiful, inline controls. Your reminder is written out like a sentence. Click an item you’d like to change and the control will re-appear. Simple.

Custom keyboards make dates, times, and repeats faster than ever.

Purpose-built Interface

Popular producitvity apps have tagging, color coding, folders, and subfolders. They call it organization. We call it bloat.

An uncompromising focus transforms your workflow into a powerful habit builder. Turn your reminders into cards. Pick a few tasks per day and make steady progress over time without even trying.

Beautiful illustrations

Your todo list can represent your goals, long-term aspirations, and your biggest celebrations. Warm, comforting illustrations help reduce your anxiety and inspire you push forward. No more boring task managers. Hello Doo.

Private by design

Privacy is a fundamental right. That’s why we don’t collect any of your data. What does that mean? No trackers, cookies, or analytics. Your data is yours and yours only.
