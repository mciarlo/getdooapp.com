Revision History

We strive to make Doo better every day. Contact us to request new illustrations, features, or if something doesn’t seem to be working correctly.

  • Doo for Mac

Version 1.2.0

  • Mac app compatibility
  • Turn off alerts for specific reminders
  • Get alerted before the due date or add reminders to your stack before they’re due
  • Preset snooze options when you long press or force press the snooze button
  • Expanded share extension support
  • Image selection improvements
  • Improved date display and illustration selection
  • Accessibility improvements
  • Bug fixes and performance improvements

Version 1.1.0

  • Skip reminders by swiping left or right
  • Prioritize your card stack from the list view
  • Adds snoozed reminders to the calendar view on iPad
  • Modified the "1 Day" snooze option to be "Until tomorrow"
  • Bug fixes and performance improvements

Version 1.0.1

  • Adds a date picker when long pressing or force pressing the snooze button
  • Fixed a bug that would prevent the Notification Center widget from displaying text properly
  • Bug fixes and performance improvements
