All features

Scheduling

  • Date presets

    Don’t have a date in mind? Select a built-in preset for tonight, tomorrow, or this weekend.

    iPhone, iPad
  • Custom repeat intervals

    Repeat reminders daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly. Set custom intervals or select specific weekdays.

  • End dates

    Repeat a reminder until a specified end date.

  • Early alerts

    Get a notification before the reminder due date. Set the early alert from a preset, or create your own. Options include minutes, hours, days, and weeks.

  • Flexible snooze

    Bypass your default snooze by long pressing or right clicking the snooze button on cards. Use force touch if your device supports it. Select from presets or choose a specific date.

  • Open-ended tasks

    Create open-ended tasks without a date or location. Perfect for those small to-dos you need to get out of your head.

  • Date-based tasks

    Create date-based tasks to be reminded at a specific time.

  • Location-based tasks

    Create location-based reminders to get reminded arriving or leaving a location. Customize the activation distance and repeat option.

  • Fast editing

    The edit screen is optimized for speed. Most controls are within distance of your thumb or mouse during editing and entry.

  • Notes

    Add URLs, addresses, and phone numbers you can open when a reminder is due.

  • Checklists

    Create checklists that appear out of your way until you need them. Manually sort tasks with drag and drop on iOS or turn on auto-sorting by status (iOS and Mac).

  • Collaboration

    Collaborate with anyone by sharing a URL. Send tasks via iMessage, SMS, email, and more. Get near real-time updates from all participants.

  • Per–reminder notifications

    Toggle notifications on a per-reminder basis. Perfect if you want a card to show up in the future, but you don’t want an alert.

  • Actionable notifications

    Complete or snooze reminders right from the notification.

    iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch
  • Nag notifications

    Receive an additional three notifications spaced ten minutes apart for any reminders that go ignored.

  • Time Zones

    Enable time zones on specific tasks that have a fixed time.

Management

  • Smart date grouping

    The list displays reminders in groups. Active items appear in the first group titled In Your Stack followed by a group for location-based tasks. The next week of upcoming tasks appear by day. Items due a week or more in the future appear grouped by month.

  • Reminder options

    On iOS, tap the "..." to show common actions like complete, copy, and share. On Mac, right click row items. Additional options like "Undo Snooze" appear within the "..." menu depending on the task status.

  • Search

    Pull down on the list view to show the search box. Find upcoming and completed reminders by title.

  • Early completion

    Complete reminders early to get them off your plate. Repeating reminders update push the alert and due date forward.

  • Priority sorting

    Sort the items in your list to set your desired task priority. The main view updates to your selected order. Perfect for preparing for the day.

  • Bulk actions

    Select multiple reminders for bulk completion and deletion.

  • Export to Clipboard

    Copy reminders to the clipboard. Copied text includes the task title, plus any notes, checklists, and due dates.

Customization

  • Morning and evening hours

    Customize the morning and evening hours. Date presets and snooze actions will use your desired hour. For example, say you set your morning hour to 8am. Snoozing a reminder until tomorrow will set that reminder to appear the next day at 8am.

  • Custom font sizing

    Choose your preferred font size: smallest, small, default, larger, or largest. There are enough size options to accommodate almost every preference or vision strength.

  • Custom font selection

    Select a font that fits you best. Choose between Avenir, San Fransisco, and Helvetica.

  • Custom theme

    Doo will adapt to your chosen iOS or Mac theme. You can choose to override the default theme on iOS to a light or dark preference.

    iPhone, iPad
  • Default snooze type

    Select a default snooze type for cards snoozed from the main card screen.

  • Completion cleanup

    Doo can automatically delete completed reminders after thirty days.

Design

  • Delight and warmth

    Doo offers a clean, inviting experience without compromising on features.

  • Natural language dates

    Date formats default to natural language for easier comprehension. For example, a task due tomorrow would display as Due tomorrow at 3:45pm instead of MM/DD/YY, 3:45pm.

  • One-handed use

    Dismiss any screen with a downward gesture.

    iPhone, iPad
  • Localized dates and times

    Doo adapts to your selected language and region settings to display the correct date and time formats.

Experience

  • No account required

    You never have to log in or create another account to use Doo. Simply download the app. As long as you have iCloud enabled on your device, you can be up and running in seconds.

  • Accessibility

    Doo includes full VoiceOver and Dynamic Type support. Buttons, headings, and items are read aloud to make interaction with the app possible without looking at the screen. Type sizes can scale to the largest supported size on iOS. Dynamic Type is not available on the Mac platform.

Integrations

  • Sync with iCloud

    Save your reminders to iCloud to sync across all your devices. Restore your tasks from iCloud if your device is ever lost or stolen.

  • Multitasking

    Use Doo with your favorite apps in Split View or Slide Over.

    iPad
  • Extension

    Add reminders from Safari, Notes, and other apps from the iOS and macOS share icon.

  • Today Widget

    View a short or long list of upcoming items. Tap a reminder to open the app with your chosen task at the top of the stack.

  • 3D Touch Shortcut

    Open Doo using the 3D Touch shortcut to load the app directly into the add interface.

    iPhone, iPad
  • URL Scheme

    Add reminders to Doo with the doo.create URL scheme. For a list of URL parameters and examples, view the documentation.

  • Keyboard Shortcuts

    Navigate Doo without a mouse. Doo includes support for tabbing through forms, switching tabs, and dismissing views.

    Mac
  • External Keyboards

    Connect an Apple Smart Keyboard or Bluetooth keyboard for a desktop-class experience on the iPad.

    iPad
  • Return Key to Save

    Speed up your workflow. Enable the return key to save reminders.

    Mac
