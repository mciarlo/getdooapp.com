A new kind of reminders app.

Transform your to-dos into a delightful stack of cards that keeps you on task and in the moment.

DownloadOn the Mac App Store

Smart features in a delightfully simple package.

Doo helps you get things done by encouraging small, achievable reminders. Each reminder you create becomes a card. This simplicity keeps you focused and on task.

  • Complete

    Mark as done

  • Snooze

    Be reminded later

  • Skip

    Send to the back

To-dos with warmth and personality.

Your reminders display one of eighteen beautiful images, each created for a particular task. This makes Doo an inviting, more engaging experience. It’s a small way to make a big difference.

More than a list

The colors and textures within each illustration add depth to your to-dos. The default images help inspire you in ways a list never could. Who said productivity apps had to be boring?

Add reminders from anywhere

Read an article, save an address, and everything in between. Use the share icon in almost any app to add a new reminder. How’s that for flexibility?

Helpful suggestions

Doo helps set you up for success with a gentle nudge if a task looks too big. You’re more likely to finish a series of smaller tasks versus one large one.

Due dates and early alerts.

Get alerts on due dates or schedule reminders early. Select from presets or create custom durations.

Flexible repeat options

Repeat daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly. Set custom intervals or choose weekdays.

Sync with iCloud

Doo saves your reminders and to–dos to iCloud Drive. Items created on one device are automatically pushed to others. And if you lose your device, your data is restored so you can pick up where you left off.

An interface that adapts to you.

Doo swaps font sizes, images, and even features to your chosen window size. You get the best experience no matter what.

Doo more with powerful extras.

  • Schedule View

    See all your reminders organized by date. Peek at what’s upcoming or look back at your history.

  • Menu Bar Mode

    Doo supports running from the menu bar. Sync and create reminders without using the dock.

  • Accessibility

    Doo supports keyboard shortcuts and Voice Over to enable an additional level of utility.
