A new take on familiar features

  • Locations

    Get reminders arriving or leaving a location. Customize the activation distance and repeat.

  • Collaboration

    Share reminders with anyone using email, iMessage, and more. Collaborate on grocery lists, trips, and more

  • Checklists

    Create lists for groceries, trips, and more. Always out of your way until you need them.

  • Customization

    Create custom repeat intervals for days, months, weeks, and years. Repeat on specific days of the week. You can even set your morning and evening hours.

  • Creation

    Enter tasks without fumbling through menus. Editing is streamlined to save you time.

  • Syncing

    Save your reminders to iCloud Drive to sync between devices. Seamlessly restore your data on any new devices.
