A smart, powerful app for reminders and to–dos.

Doo helps you get things done by encouraging small, achievable reminders. Each reminder you create becomes a card. Swipe cards away to mark them completed and power through your to-do list.

Simple by design

Doo presents your reminders as a stack of cards, making it easier to focus on one task at a time. It also enables an innovative user interface you that can use one-handed.

  • Complete

    Drag cards up to mark them completed.

  • Snooze

    Drag cards down to snooze them for later.

Doo more with powerful features.

  • Add to Doo

    Read a saved article or cook a delicious recipe. Add to-dos from Safari, Twitter, and more with the Doo share extension.

  • Smart suggestions

    You’ll get a gentle nudge if a task looks too big. You’re more likely to finish small, single objective tasks.

  • Customizable workflow

    Work on your own schedule with snooze durations of 1 day, 1 hour, or 15 minutes. Doo will always send a new alert for time sensitive reminders.

Bold illustrations. A fun way to power through the day.

Each reminder displays one of eighteen beautiful images created for a unique task. This adds a whole new dimension to your to–dos and helps you recognize tasks more quickly. It’s a small way of making a big difference.

  • More than a list

    The colors and textures in each illustration bring warmth to your to–dos in ways a list never could. Who said reminders had to be boring?

Made for you. And all your devices.

Doo saves your reminders and to–dos to iCloud Drive. Items created on one device are automatically pushed to the others. And if you lose your device, all your data is restored so you can pick up where you left off.

Today Widget

Add Doo to your Notification Center to see your top three reminders or to add another.

3D Touch Shortcut

Doo takes advantage of the latest iOS technologies. Deep press the app icon to add a new reminder.

Accessibility

Doo includes full VoiceOver support. Take advantage of every feature without looking at the screen.

Get Doo. Get things done.

It turns out the perfect to–do list isn’t a list but something much more. Intelligent features and attention to detail make Doo a smart, fun way to get things done.
