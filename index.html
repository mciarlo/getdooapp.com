Gestures

Swipe away cards to prepare for your day

Gestures turn your entire screen into a button. Select your tasks for the day by swiping away cards or use the list view.

Task Creation

Creating a task is as easy as typing the title

Doo uses a custom set of keyboards to show calendars, weekdays, and all the tools you need to set up tasks without any fuss.

Collaboration

Send tasks to others with iMessage for easy collaboration

Customization

Customize notifications

Powerful per reminder notification settings make it easy to disable native alerts, set time zones, and repeat from the completion date. Use custom repeat durations for days, weeks, weekdays, months, and years.

Integrations

Works where you are on Apple Watch

Manage tasks from the Apple Watch app or review from using the watch face complications. Two glanceable options give you your next due task or your last due item.

Private by design

Keeps your data private

Privacy is a fundamental right. That’s why Doo includes screen locking. No "Pro" upgrade required. We also don’t collect your data. What does this mean? No trackers, no cookies, and no analytics. Simple — the way it should be.
