The todo app with
invisible superpowers.

Your to-do list is a stack of cards. Beautiful illustrations make your reminders inviting and approachable. An uncompromising focus transforms your workflow into a powerful habit builder. Doo is the get things done app with invisible superpowers.
Never use a productivity app again.

Feature name

Popular producitvity apps have tagging, color coding, folders, and subfolders. They call it organization. We call it bloat. Turn your reminders into cards. organize cards into groups. Pick a few tasks per day and make steady progress over time without even trying.
