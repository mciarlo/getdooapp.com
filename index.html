How it works

Major Features

Effortless task entry

Creating a task by typing in the title. Tap an icon above the keyboard to set dates, locations, notes, and more. Tap a word to edit. Simple.

Replay

Today Mode

Tap the Today button to select your tasks for the day. Doo will snooze the rest so you can improve your focus and avoid productivity guilt.

Replay

Designed with purpose

Popular producitvity apps have tagging, color coding, folders, and subfolders. They call it organization. We call it bloat. Doo is everything you need and nothing you don’t.

Keeps your data private

Privacy is a fundamental right. That’s why Doo includes screen locking. No "Pro" upgrade required. We also don’t collect your data. What does this mean? No trackers, no cookies, and no analytics. Simple — the way it should be.
