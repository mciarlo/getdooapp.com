Key features

An acclaimed design

Doo helps you make the most of your time with thoughtful details. Every interaction and element is designed for speed, warmth, and reliability.

Collaborate with others

Share cards with others using iCloud. Invite people using email, text, Airdrop, and more. Revoke access with the tap of a button.

Create checklists

Create grocery lists or pack for a trip. Doo makes it easy to create checklists that are out of the way until you need them.

Be reminded at a location

Get reminders when arriving or leaving a location. Customize the activation distance and repeat options.

Customizable date and repeat options

Doo has powerful functionality built-in. Repeat on specific weekdays and custom intervals. Select end dates. You can even turn off notifications for single reminders. Simplicity meets power.

Sync with iCloud

Doo saves your reminders and to–dos to iCloud Drive. Items created on one device are automatically pushed to others. And if you lose your device, your data is restored so you can pick up where you left off.
